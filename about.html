About

Suprasegmental Reader is a project developed by Shanti der Blätter as part of a research study under the direction of Mark Tanner, Department of Linguistics and English Language at Brigham Young University.

Shanti is pursuing a bachelor's degree in linguistics with minors in computer science and TESOL (Teaching English to Speakers of Other Languages). This project particularly appealed to her as it combined her loves of language, teaching, and coding.

The code for this site is available on GitHub. Please fork, star, and contribute!
